Why Choose North Point?
Our Team of Property Managers, Leasing Specialists, and Property Consultants provide the knowledge and expertise to get your property rented out at its highest market value.
From finding your property a qualified corporate tenant to managing your real estate assets, our team of professionals will guide you through every step of the process.
Property Management
Tenant Placement
All potential tenants are screened ahead of time to provide you with high quality AAA tenants. Prescreening includes documents such as government IDs, credit report, income verification, references, etc.
Financial Reporting
You will be able to track the condition of your investment with our annual inspections. These visits allow you to stay up to date on the condition of your investment and helps us to be proactive in the maintenance of your property.
Rent Collection
Here at North Point Realty we collect rent for you. Tenants can pay rent to us via our digital platforms. Direct deposit and bill payments to you make it easy for you to keep track of your earnings.
Tenant Eviction
We have a in-house legal department who evaluates your case, to focus on evicting the tenant as quickly as possible. We navigate Landlord and Tenant Board paperwork for you.
Maintenance & Repairs
You will no longer need to worry about taking care of maintenance issues on your own. We will coordinate all the repairs using licensed contractors to make sure the job gets done right the first time.
Lease Renewals
As we approach the end of your current lease with our tenant. Our team of professionals will proactively start searching for another suitable tenant so we don't experience any vacancies.
Tenant Guarantee
If the tenant we provide were to ever break the lease within the lease term, we guarantee another qualified tenant free of charge.
