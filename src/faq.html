All of our tenants are prescreened ahead of time by our Leasing Department. How do you know what my property is worth?
Our knowledge in the GTA rental market is able to accurately appraise any property in every area. We also look at comparable units in your building and area. With our tenants' budgets in mind we are able to help you get the highest market value for your property.
Where do you service?

How much do you charge for tenant placement?
We charge a standard fee of one month's rent out of the year for leasing out your property. This fee is not due until the lease agreement is signed by our tenant and you receive both first and last month's rent deposit.
Will I be able to see/speak to the tenant?
Of course! Before you accept an offer from any of our applicants, you are able to request a virtual meeting via Zoom or Google Meet.
How long is your Tenant Placement Service Agreement?
Our contract period is only 15 days. If we cannot find you a tenant within that timeframe, our agreement terminates.
How much do you charge for Property Management?
We provide different service packages for property management.

Our Silver package highlights Tenant Guarantee (cost: 2% monthly rent).

Our Gold package (most popular) highlights the core of our property management service (cost 5% monthly rent).

What happens at the end of the lease period?
At the end of the lease period, if our tenant decides to stay, we do not charge you for subsequent years. If you are under property management, we are able to help you find another tenant without any vacancies.
